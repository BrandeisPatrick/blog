Hello, world

June 8, 2026

I've kept private notes for years — scattered across apps, half-finished, never seen by anyone. This is me trying the opposite: writing in public, in plain text, on a page I fully control.

The rules I'm giving myself

Keep it small. Keep it fast. Don't let tooling get in the way of actually writing. Every post is just an HTML file — no database, no build pipeline, nothing to break in two years when I come back to it.

The best blog is the one you'll still be posting to next year.

What goes here

Notes on things I'm building, things I'm reading, and the occasional rabbit hole. If any of it is useful to you, that's a bonus.
